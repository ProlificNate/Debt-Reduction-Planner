Frequently Asked Questions

What is Debt Dedox?

Debt Dedox is a debt reduction planner that helps users manage and reduce their debt through personalized plans and actionable insights.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and providing your personal details.

Is my data secure on Debt Dedox?

Yes, we prioritize data security and use AWS security services, including IAM and Shield, to protect your information.

Technical Questions

How does Debt Dedox calculate my debt reduction plan?

Debt Dedox uses algorithms that analyze your debt, income, and expenses to create a customized repayment plan.

What browsers are supported?

Debt Dedox supports the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
